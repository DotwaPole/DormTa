Our Story

DormTa! started from a simple frustration: students in Cebu struggled to find safe, affordable, and verified dormitories near their schools. Endless Facebook groups, unverified listings, and last-minute scrambles were the norm.

We built DormTa! as a centralized, tech-driven platform where students can browse verified accommodations, compare prices, and connect with landlords — all in one place. Our mission is to make the transition to university life easy and stress-free.

For Students

Access a curated list of verified dormitories and apartments. Use our virtual tours and secure communication tools to find your perfect match without ever having to leave your current desk.

For Landlords

Reach thousands of active students looking for a place to stay. Our powerful property management tools allow you to list, manage, and fill your vacancies more efficiently than ever before.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account or list a property. This data is processed securely using Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to connect students with landlords, display listings, and improve our platform. Your information is stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore and is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We use Google Firebase for real-time updates and secure data storage. Firebase complies with industry security standards to protect your information.

4. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, email us at dormta7@gmail.com or reach out to our team.

Help Center & FAQ

Everything you need to know about finding and booking your dorm safely.

1

How do I book a dorm?

Booking with DormTa! is simple and designed for students:

  • Compare prices, amenities, and proximity.
  • Click on a listing to see the landlord's contact details.
  • Contact the landlord directly to schedule a viewing or reserve a slot.
2

Is DormTa! free for students?

Yes! DormTa! is 100% free for students searching for housing. We want to make your university transition as smooth as possible.
